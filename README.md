# Hey, I'm Yannik

Backend developer with a focus on Java and Spring Boot. I like building systems that are clean, well-structured, and built to last.

Out of curiosity I regularly pick up other languages and frameworks to play around with. Over the years this has led me to explore Python, C++, Rust, Vue.js, and React. I also keep one eye on cybersecurity, privacy, and the hacking scene.

---

## What I'm into right now

- **Bitcoin Core**: working my way through the codebase to understand what's actually happening under the hood
- **AI-assisted workflows**: exploring creative ways to automate repetitive tasks and integrate LLMs into development workflows
- **[Elysian Fantasy Map](https://github.com/trusty-fire/Elysian-Fantasy-Map)**: an interactive world map for my sister's novel, built with Leaflet.js. Equal parts side project and labour of love.
